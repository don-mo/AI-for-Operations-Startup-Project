OpDecision AI

Your personalized AI-Powered Decision Support.

Robbie and Don's vision is to maintain a company's knowledge base when it comes to operations decision-making. Robbie's grandpa is a business owner and knows exactly how much to order of each product to keep in stock, when to run promotions, and how to price. But what happens when he retires? We want to capture that intuition and make it available to the next generation of operators, so that they can make informed decisions and keep the business running smoothly.
